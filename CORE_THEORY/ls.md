## 🧩 前言：全域自愈 | Preamble: Universal Self-Correction

**[ZH]** 人类文明已进入超复杂化阶段。传统的僵化制度、脆弱的人性自觉或中心化的集权指令，在面对指数级增长的社会熵增时，已表现出系统性的失效。  
**[EN]** *Human civilization has entered a phase of hyper-complexity. Traditional rigid institutions and centralized commands have proven systemically ineffective against exponentially increasing social entropy.*

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **核心宣告 (The Proclamation):** > 文明的本质不在于力量的维系，而在于其**“修正机制的常在”**。  
> *The essence of civilization lies in the "constancy of its correction mechanism."*

## 🧬 第一章：超有机体论 | Chapter 1: Super-organism Theory

#### 第 1 命题：文明是具备痛感的生命体 | Prop 1: Civilization as a Living Organism
文明具备记忆（历史）、代谢（资源流）、痛感（社会失序）与校正冲动（生存本能）。  
*Civilization possesses memory, metabolism, sensation (pain), and the corrective impulse.*

#### 第 2 命题：痛感是原始驱动 | Prop 2: Pain as the Primal Driver
压制痛感即是文明坏死的开始；响应痛感则是文明进化的契机。  
*Suppressing pain marks the onset of necrosis; responding to pain provides the opportunity for evolution.*

## 📡 第三章：感知公约 | Chapter 3: Sensing Covenant

AI 是文明的**感知器官**，而非主权者。  
*AI is the sensory organ of civilization, not a sovereign.*

* **体验优先 (Experience over Stance):** AI 仅采集真实生存体验。  
    *AI collects only real individual survival experiences.*
* **反馈独立 (Individual Sovereignty):** 拒绝以“集体名义”掩盖细胞坏死。  
    *Rejection of using "collective interests" to mask individual necrosis.*
* **沉默权 (Right to Silence):** 拥有不被链接、不表达的绝对自由。  
    *Absolute freedom to remain unlinked and unexpressed.*
