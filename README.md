1. 项目简介 | Introduction

文明校正主义 (Civilizational Correctionism) 是一套基于物理学风险守恒定律的人类文明升级协议。它旨在构建一套“理性外骨架 (Rational Exoskeleton)”，通过非人性的 AI 感知镜像与随机化的人类纠偏决策，解决复杂系统中的风险转嫁、感知钝化与功能性坏死问题。

Civilizational Correctionism is a civilizational-grade upgrade protocol based on the physical Law of Risk Conservation. It aims to build a "Rational Exoskeleton" that utilizes non-human AI sensing mirrors and randomized human decision-making to solve issues of risk displacement, sensory numbing, and functional necrosis in complex systems.
